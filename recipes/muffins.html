Description

Incredibly delicious chocolate Muffins, they are not only easy to make they're quick to make as well

Ingredients

  • 2 dl caster sugar
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar
  • 2 dl wheat flour (2 dl corresponds to 120 g)
  • 1 dl cocoa
  • an egg
  • 1 dl milk
  • 1 dl netural canola oil
  • 1 dl hot water
  • 10 muffin tins

What to Do

  1. Set the oven to 175Â°C.
  2. Mix sugar, baking powder, salt and vanilla sugar in a bowl.
  3. Sift in flour and cocoa.
  4. Whisk together eggs, milk and oil quickly in another bowl. (Important that it is a completely neutral oil.) Stir in the dry ingredients preferably with a balloon whisk.
  5. Add the hot water, little by little. Mix to a smooth batter.
  6. Divide the batter into the muffin tins placed in a muffin tray.
  7. Bake in the middle of the oven for about 25 minutes. Feel with a test stick so that the muffins are dry in the middle. Remove and let cool on a wire rack.
