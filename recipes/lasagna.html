Description

A tasty Italian dish that is impossible to dislike and very easy to make ao easy in fat that a child could do it (under adult suprvision of course)

Ingredients

  • 2 yellow onions
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 500 grams of ground beef(or preferred alternative)
  • 1 tbsp of cooking oil
  • 4 tbsp of tomato puree
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp drie rosemary
  • 1 pack crushed tomatoes
  • 1 meatstock cube
  • Desired amount of salt and pepper

    • Cheese Sauce

  • 6 tbsp butter(approx. 90g)
  • 6 tbsp wheat flour
  • 10 dl milk
  • 2 dl grated parmesan

  • 9 lasagna plates

What to Do

  1. Set oven to 175℃
  2. Peel and chop onions and garlic. Saute minced meat, onion and garlic in the oil in a frying pan.
  3. Add tomato puree that can be sautéed in the mince for a minute or so. Season with thyme and rosemary. Add crushed tomatoes and bouillon cube let it boil for about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Melt the butter and whisk in the flour. Pour in a little of the milk at a time while whisking. Bring to a boil and let the sauce cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in the parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Pour some cheese sauce into the bottom of an ovenproof dish (approx. 20 x 30 cm). Add a layer of lasagna tiles and then a layer of minced meat sauce. Repeat the steps and finish with a layer of cheese sauce.
  6. Place the lasagna in the middle of the oven for about 40 minutes (or the time according to the packaging on the lasagna plates.)

And now serve with preferred veggies and something to drink
